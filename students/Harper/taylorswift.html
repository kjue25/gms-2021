Taylor Allison Swift was born December 13, 1989, she is an American singer-songwriter, record producer, music video director, philanthropist, and actress.She has sold more than 50 million albums—including 37 million in the US, she is one of the world's best-selling music artists and the highest-earning female musician of the 2010s. She has won 10 Grammy Awards, an Emmy Award, six Guinness world records and is the most-awarded act and woman at the American Music Awards.

Taylor Swift has released 56 singles as lead artist, 7 singles as a featured artist, and 28 promotional singles
