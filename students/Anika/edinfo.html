Ed 101

Early Life & Career

Ed Sheeran. An international pop star. Edward Christopher Sheeran, his real name. Born February 17, 1991, this very special ginger grew up in Halifax, West Yorkshire, in England. At just 14 years old, Ed moved out of home and went to London to pursue his dreams of being a singer/songwriter. He grew up wealthy, and still found a way to become even more rich! What a king. Irish blood running through his veins, this british singer is considered one of the most iconic gingers around. He started off playing small gigs in London and Cambridge. He created a YouTube channel and his fan base expanded quickly. In 2011, Ed was signed to Asylum Records after becoming popular on the internet. He began writing lots of popular songs for popular groups and singers. For example, he was a co-writer on "Moments" by One Direction. He soon became an international success. On March 3, 2017, Ed may have just released the most iconic album ever made by him. "Divide". His songs "Shape of You", "Perfect", and "Thinking Out Loud" became very popular from that album.
