Dwarven traits

Dwarves are often skilled wrriors, miners or workers of rock and metal. They usualy stand under 5 feet tall and can weigh as much as a human that is 2 feet taller that them. Their skin has tones is similar to that of the earth. They usualy leave their hair long and in simple styles. Dwarves can live to be 400 years old. Dwarves usualy go on quests to ataine gold or riches.
