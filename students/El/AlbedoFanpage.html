Albedo

Albedo is a 5 star character in the game Genshin Impact.

He was released back in December and has not gotten a rerun yet. Most other 5 star characters that were released between the launch of the game and March have gotten reruns

Backstory

There is not much known about Albedo's backsory, as he is very myterious. What we do know is that he is not from Mondstadt which is the region we meet him in. Many people theorise that he was created with alchemy. Though this is not comfirmed, there is lots of evidence to back it up. (Ignore my last sentence it was confrimed. He's not human. He's also related to the poisonous dragon Durin, who died on the snowy mountain known as Dragonspine.)
