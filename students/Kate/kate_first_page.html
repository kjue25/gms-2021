Different Types Of Cells

Animal Cells, Plant cells, Human cells (red and white), those are a few types but ther are a lot more

Plant cells

Plant cells like every other cell has cell walls. The thing that sets it apart from other cells is that its walls are made up of composed cellulose.

Animal cells

Animal cells are a little different than plant cells for various reasons one being its shape. Its shape is more sphere shaped while plant cells are kinda more elevated but flat. They help the tissue and organ of the animal to function and help the body in general to continue.

Human specific cells

white blood cells

red blood cells

although red and white blood cells are both found in the human body, they both have different functions.the red blood cell is in charge of carrying blood and spreading it throughout the body. They can also be found in bone marrowsand because of them bones stay alive.
