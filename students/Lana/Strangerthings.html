All About Stranger Things

By: Lana Weshahy

Stranger Things is a suspensful, and thrilling show. It currently has 3 seasons but the 4th is being released sometime next year in 2022. I recomend watching stranger things (you can easily find it on netflix) if you enjoy watching a suspensful and scary show.

WARNING: If you get nightmares very easily I do not recomend watching Stranger Things

The first season of Stranger Things is about Will Byers going missing. As Will's freinds try to find Will Byers they uncover another secret.

In the second season of Stranger Things the mindflayer takes over Wills body. Will's mom and her new boyfriend take Will to get treated at a doctor. But while there he unleashes something new.

Stranger things season 3 is about how there is a brand new mall with hidden secrets of its own. Labs are trying to reopen the gate but unleash something way worse.
