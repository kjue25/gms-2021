PINK

All about the color pink

Pink is basically red mixed with white. Pink comes in many different shades including hot pink, baby pink, etc.

Why pink is a good color

Pink is a really good color because it is very pretty. It can make anything look good, and it goes with a lot of things and other colors, like purple, yellow, green, blue, white, etc.
