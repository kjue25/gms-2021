Genshin Impact is an open world RPG created by miHoYo, a Chinese game and animation studio originating in Shanghai. It was released in September 2020, and over the course of about one year, it has been downloaded over 40 million times, with around 3 million active users per day. They have won multiple awards, been featured on the app store, and they currently have a total revenue of around 1.9 billion USD.
