Artfer School

Art Work


Unit 1: In unit one, we make Cubist art. We study Pablo Picasso and other artists who do Cubism.


Unit 2: In our second unit, we make Abstract-Expressionist art. For these classes, we get messy and have fun with Jackson Pollock.


Unit 3: For unit three, our students play with Van Gogh. They make Post-Impressionist collage and 3D art .


Unit 4: Our classes work with Frida Khalo to make self portraits. While working with paint, oil pastel, and brush tip markers, they create Symbolist art.
